# Execution Roadmap And KPI

## 1. Current State

As of 2026-03-08, the base system is already past the original MVP skeleton.

Implemented:
- 3 entry flows
- decision engine with tier, urgency, scope, and branch outputs
- noindex result page
- pSEO public surface with 98 pages
- trust hub and trust pages
- regional and authority families
- lead capture and admin read side
- structured search metadata
- core decision-doc depth on 12 high-intent pages

## 2. Current North Star

Primary operating metric:
- completed decision sessions

Public-surface supporting metrics:
- tool handoff click-through rate
- internal compare click-through rate
- lead submit rate
- result view to CTA click rate

## 3. What Matters Next

### Priority 1. Deepen winner pages
Goal:
- turn the strongest pages into durable decision documents

Work:
- expand the core 12 pattern into the next wave only after observing usage
- add scenario-specific FAQ and confusion blocks where clusters justify it
- keep public pages closer in value to the noindex result pages

### Priority 2. Expand scenario compare depth
Goal:
- make compare pages earn future partner links

Work:
- add more compare pages tied to specific problems, not generic product classes
- strengthen claim-check logic in compare pages
- keep compare pages downstream from testing and verification logic

### Priority 3. Expand regional clusters selectively
Goal:
- grow state coverage only where local geology or rules materially change the next step

Work:
- add states by cluster economics, not by state count vanity
- pair each regional page with matching compare and authority pages

### Priority 4. Make partner activation easy later
Goal:
- be partner-ready without turning public pages into vendor pages now

Work:
- keep internal compare pages as the active commercial bridge
- preserve disclosure and evidence-first ordering
- define partner gating by cluster and branch

## 4. KPI Set

### Acquisition KPI
- indexed page count by family
- family-level click-through rate from search console
- page-level internal handoff rate

### Product KPI
- entry mode selected
- test completed
- result viewed
- scenario compare engagement
- trust page visitation from public pages

### Monetization KPI
- lead submit rate
- lead by family
- lead by slug
- compare page click-through rate
- revenue per qualified session after partner activation

### Quality KPI
- unsupported rate
- stale sample rate
- low-confidence rate
- retest-first recommendation share
- red and amber branch distribution

## 5. What Not To Do Next

Do not prioritize:
- broad generic affiliate pages
- flat 50-state expansion with little local delta
- top-of-page vendor pushes on public pages
- adding more weak pages before deepening winner clusters

## 6. Current Practical Sequence

1. deepen the current high-intent cluster set
2. expand scenario compare pages where the next action is already credible
3. add partner channels only after the compare path is strong enough
4. expand regional coverage where the local delta is real

That sequence keeps growth, trust, and monetization aligned.
